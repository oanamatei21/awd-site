Hi there! :)

I'm Oana Matei, a math enthusiast who embarked on the journey of mathematics during high school. From competing in contests to pursuing a master's in Advanced Mathematics, my passion has only grown day by day. Inspired by my grandmother, a dedicated math teacher, I found my calling in education. Teaching isn't just a job, it's a way to share the joy of discovery and the endless possibilities within mathematics.

I prioritize practical learning in my teaching approach. This ensures students not only grasp theoretical concepts but also acquire the practical skills essential for academic and professional success.

Outside of teaching, my interests add depth to my character. I find joy in singing and drawing, expressing my creative side. Traveling is a passion that not only satisfies my wanderlust but also enriches my understanding of diverse cultures.

As a perpetual learner, I'm constantly exploring new subjects and skills. These varied interests shape who I am, and I'm excited to share these facets with my students.
